About OasisLife

Welcome to OasisLife! This website is designed to help people find peace and control in their lives. We don't want this site to only be a get help site, we want people to able to talk about their struggles in their life and find people who have or going through the same thing as them. We want to let you guys know that you're not alone and there will always be people on OasisLife to help you through the tough times. This is a safe place and you guys can be you true selves here. You don't need to always talk about your struggles, you can talk about anything you're interested in. :)

Sajara Magdaleno

I am a 16 year old who lives in Illinois. I am currently in a program called Girls Who Code and we learned how to code with many different programming languages. My group and I decided to make this website ourselves because we thought it would be nice to see people be able to talk about their feelings. I do have family members who have anxiety and it's hard to see them struggle with it because they have no one they can talk to/ no one there to understand what they are going through. I hear my parents talking about my cousin (who has anxiety) sometimes, and my parents say that my cousin is overthinking things and that he needs to relax. I don't think that what they say that he should do will help him because he has tried to 'relax' before, but that doesn't work. I feel like this website can help people, like my cousin, find some peace. I also think that this website can teach people, like my parents, things about mental health.
